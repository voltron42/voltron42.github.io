D&D -- races and classes

Races

The original classes of D&D -- Human, halfling, gnome, dwarf, half-orc, elf, half-elf

Tiefling

Tieflings are the result of the bond between a human and a demonic entity, giving them certain infernal powers.

Dragonborn

Dragonborn are people with dragon-like features and abilities. Colors and corresponding abilities are similar to that of actual dragons.

Classes

Barbarian

Barbarians are warriors with a berserker rage which gives them temporary power bonuses.

Bard

Bards are a versatile class, capable of both physical combat and magic use. Their magic is based in their artistic, musical and creative talents.

Cleric

Clerics are powerful healers with divinely-granted abilities over life or death, as well as power to repel or control the undead.

Druid

Druids are use nature-themed magic, particularly linked to plants, animals, and the elements. They can also assume the form of various types of animals.

Fighter

Fighter is a generic, catch all class for all manor of physical combatent: Bodyguards, adventurers, former soldiers, invading bandit kings, or master swordsmen.

Wizard

Wizards are magic users whose abilities are learnt, meaning they are derived from one of the "schools of magic": Alteration, Illusion, Enchantment, Divination, Conjuration, Invocation, Necromancy, Abjuration

Monk

Monks are a special type of martial arts fighter given certain advantages by their chosen "monastic tradition".

Paladin

Paladins are warriors of the divine which are similar to clerics, but less powerful in those abilities.

Ranger

Hunter, tracker, survivalist, animal handler -- Bear Grylls meets Jack Hannah.

Rogue

Thief, spy, assassin -- Basically ye olde 007.

Sorcerer

Magic users like wizards, but with an inherent connection the arcane energies. These connections can be tenuous.

Warlock

Warlocks are the anti-clerics; where a cleric's power is granted by a divine entity, a warlock's power comes from a pact to an extra-planar patron, either an Archfey or a demon.

Custom

D&D settings tend to be high fantasy, but if there is something or someone you want to be that isn't listed, talk to your DM.
